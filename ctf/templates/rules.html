Competition Rules

  1. Groups may not communicate with other groups.
  2. Groups may not in any way provide aid or support to other groups.
  3. Talking between team members must be kept at a low volume to allow other groups to think in peace.
  4. Groups must not show or share their screens to other groups.
  5. Groups may use any online or tangible resource during the competition but must not share these resources with other groups.
  6. A flag is not considered achieved until successfully submitted via this website.
