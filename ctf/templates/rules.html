Competition Rules

  1. Teams may not communicate with other groups.
  2. Teams may not in any way provide aid or support to other teams.
  3. Talking between team members must be kept at a low volume to allow other teams to think in peace.
  4. Teams must not show or share their screens to other teams.
  5. Groups may use any online or tangible resources during the competition but must not share these resources with other groups.
  6. A flag is not considered achieved until successfully submitted via this website.
***Violation of any of these rules above will eliminate a team from competition despite the result displayed on the scoreboard! {% endblock %}
